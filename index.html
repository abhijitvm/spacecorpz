Who We Are

SPACECORPZ Industries is more than just a startup; it's the embodiment of a bold vision to explore the cosmos, advance aviation technologies, and push the boundaries of defense and AI innovation. Registered under MSME in India, SPACECORPZ Industries is driven by a group of passionate innovators specializing in aviation, defense technology, software technology, and AI.

Our Expertise

Aerospace Innovation

We are at the forefront of developing new technologies for space exploration and aerospace applications. Our projects include advanced research in solar sailing, light sail technology, and other cutting-edge aerospace solutions.

Defence Technology

Our team is dedicated to enhancing defense capabilities through the development of advanced military technologies. We focus on creating solutions that improve security and operational efficiency in critical environments.

AI and Software

Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and software technology, we create smart systems that adapt to the evolving needs of our clients. Our innovations in AI help drive progress across various sectors.

Drone and UAV Solutions

We offer specialized courses in drones and unmanned aerial vehicles, designed to educate and recruit the next generation of experts. These courses play a crucial role in supporting our growth and development.
